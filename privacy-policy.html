Privacy Policy for Puzzle Math Puzzle App by Takksemd

We do not collect or store any personal information about you or your use of the app.

There is no sign-up or log-in.
No analytics, and no third-party analytics.
No advertisement, and no third-party advertisement.
No location info.
No internet access.
No cookies.

The following sentence is included purely for bureacratic and legal reasons: We do not share this non-existant personal information with any third-parties.

The puzzles are only stored locally on your device. If you lose your phone or tablet, there is unfortunately no way for us to recreate the puzzles you have started or completed. Apple or Google may themselves back up this information for you, but the backup information (if it exists) is never sent to us.
